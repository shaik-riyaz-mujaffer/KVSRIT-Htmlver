Overview

Administrative Structure

The administration of KVSRIT is organized to support the academic mission of the institute. Under the guidance of the Chairman, Secretary & Correspondent, and Principal, various administrative departments work cohesively to ensure that students, faculty, and staff have the resources and support they need to excel.

Departments

Administrative Departments

Accounts & Finance

Handles fee collection, salary disbursement, budgeting, financial audits, and all monetary transactions of the institution.

Student Affairs

Manages student welfare, grievance redressal, anti-ragging measures, counseling services, and co-curricular activities.

Examination Branch

Coordinates with JNTUA for examination scheduling, hall ticket generation, result publication, and revaluation processes.

Library & Resources

Manages the central library with 25,000+ volumes, digital resources, e-journals, and NPTEL video lecture access.

IT & Infrastructure

Maintains campus network, computer labs, smart classrooms, ERP systems, and website management with 24/7 internet access.

Hostel & Transport

Administers separate hostels for boys and girls with mess facilities, and a fleet of college buses covering major routes.
