Training & Placement Cell

Bridging the gap between academia and industry, ensuring our students are career-ready.

Our Vision

To effectivley promote the students to meet the industry expectations and to create a platform where they can showcase their skills and talent. To be a center of excellence in training and placement by imparting technical and soft skills to the students.

Our Mission

To achieve 100% placement for eligible students. To guide students in choosing the right career path. To provide opportunities for holistic development of students. To facilitate industry-institute interaction.

Core Objectives

Our strategic goals to ensure student success in the professional world.

1

Skill Development

Organizing training programs to improve soft skills, technical skills, and aptitude.

2

Industry Readiness

Bridging the gap between industry and academia through internships and industrial visits.

3

Campus Drives

Inviting reputed companies for campus recruitment and facilitating the selection process.

4

Career Guidance

Providing counseling and guidance to students regarding career opportunities.

5

Alumni Interaction

Facilitating interaction with alumni to understand industry trends and expectations.

6

Entrepreneurship

Encouraging students to take up entrepreneurship and providing necessary support.
