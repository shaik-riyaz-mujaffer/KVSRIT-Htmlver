Introduction

About DRKVSRIT

Dr. K. V. Subba Reddy Institute of Technology is promoted by Vaibhav Educational Society (VES) with the motto of “Work is Worship“. Our prime objective is to offer Quality Education for the Betterment of Society.

We persistently seek and adopt innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education on a consistent basis. The campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere, attracting students from all corners of Andhra Pradesh.

Our Strength

Our Memoranda of Understanding with various Industries are our major strength. Many of our students bring high quality to their work and add value and esteem to their organizations. With steady steps, we continue our march forward.

History of DRKVSRIT

Established in 2007, DRKVSRIT is affiliated to JNTUA, Ananthapuramu, and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The College is headed by its Founder and Chairman, Dr. K. V. Subba Reddy.
In recognition of his outstanding service, he was conferred with the Jewel of India award and the Life Time Achievement Gold Medal Award.
