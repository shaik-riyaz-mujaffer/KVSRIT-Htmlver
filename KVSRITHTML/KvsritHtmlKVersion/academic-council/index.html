About

What is the Academic Council?

The Academic Council is the principal academic body of KVSRIT, constituted as per the norms of JNTUA and AICTE. It is responsible for the maintenance of standards of instruction, education, and examination within the institute. The council advises the Governing Body on all academic matters and ensures continuous improvement in the quality of education.

Responsibilities

Roles & Responsibilities

Curriculum Development

Approve and review course curricula, syllabi, and academic regulations in accordance with JNTUA guidelines and industry requirements.

Quality Assurance

Monitor and maintain standards of teaching, learning, and evaluation processes to ensure continual quality improvement.

Examination Policies

Frame and review examination policies, evaluation criteria, grading systems, and academic integrity measures.

Research Promotion

Encourage and facilitate research activities, faculty development programs, and industry-institute collaborations.

Meeting Schedule

The Academic Council meets at least twice in an academic year — once at the beginning of each semester. Special meetings may be convened by the Chairman as required to address urgent academic matters.

Minutes of all meetings are documented and made available to stakeholders through the institutional quality assurance cell (IQAC).
