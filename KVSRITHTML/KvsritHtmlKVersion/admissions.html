How to Apply

Admissions at KVSRIT are strictly based on merit and the guidelines set by the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE).

Category A (70%)

Convener Quota

Filled through centralized counseling based on ranks secured in EAMCET / ECET / ICET / PGECET.

Visit APSCHE Portal

Category B (30%)

Management Quota

Filled directly by the college management based on merit and as per APSCHE guidelines.
