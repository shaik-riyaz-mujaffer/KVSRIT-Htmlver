Service Rules & Regulations

Guidelines governing the conduct and service conditions at KVSRIT.

The service rules of Dr. K.V. Subba Reddy Institute of Technology (KVSRIT) are formulated to ensure smooth functioning of the institution and to provide a conducive environment for teaching, learning, and research.

Note

These rules are subject to change based on the amendments made by the Governing Body from time to time. Employees are advised to check with the administration for the latest updates.

1. General Conditions of Service

  • All appointments are made based on merit and as per the guidelines of AICTE/JNTUA.
  • Employees shall be on probation for a period of one year from the date of joining.
  • Resignation requires a notice period of one to three months depending on the cadre.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Every employee shall maintain absolute integrity and devotion to duty.
  • Employees must be punctual and regular in their attendance.
  • Any form of harassment or discrimination is strictly prohibited.

3. Leave Rules

  • Casual Leaves (CL): 15 days per calendar year.
  • Academic Leaves (AL): Granted for attending conferences, workshops, etc.
  • Maternity Leave: As per government norms.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

Teaching Staff

Responsible for academic delivery, student mentoring, research activities, and administrative duties as assigned.

Non-Teaching Staff

Responsible for administrative support, laboratory maintenance, and ensuring smooth day-to-day operations.
